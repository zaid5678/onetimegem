For Brands & Business

A corporate event is a version of your brand made physical. The venue choice, the table setting, the way guests move through the room — all of it communicates something about the business behind it. We understand that, and we plan accordingly.


We work with brands on dinners, launches, conferences, and away-days — managing every element from the initial brief through to the final handshake at the end of the evening.

We handle

Venue sourcing Shortlisting, visiting, and securing the right space — whether that's a private dining room in Mayfair or a converted warehouse in East London.
Styling & design Translating a brand or brief into the physical details of a room — layout, lighting, floral, table settings, branded elements.
Production AV, staging, lighting, and technical requirements — coordinated with your venue and briefed to every supplier.
Guest management Invitation logistics, RSVP tracking, dietary requirements, seating plans, and arrivals management on the day.
On-site coordination A calm, experienced team on the ground from setup through to close — managing suppliers, keeping to schedule, and handling whatever comes up.
Supplier liaison Single point of contact for every supplier — catering, AV, floristry, entertainment, security, transport — so your team isn't fielding fifteen separate conversations.

The kind of work we do

Product launches in Shoreditch · Investor dinners in Mayfair · Team off-sites in the Cotswolds · Brand activations in Brixton · Private screenings in the City · Leadership conferences in central London · Summer parties across the South East.
