What we Do

01

Weddings

We plan and coordinate weddings that feel completely personal — to the ceremony space, the seating cards tied in silk, the schedule that gives you room to breathe. Whether you need someone with you from the very beginning or a calm pair of hands on the day itself, we work with each couple to find the level of support that fits.

Full planning, partial planning, and day-of coordination. Tailored to your venue, your suppliers, and the version of the day you've been imagining.

Read more about wedding planning

02

Corporate

From a brand dinner for forty to a conference for several hundred, we manage every moving part — venue sourcing, production, guest management, supplier liaison, and on-site coordination. We understand that a corporate event reflects the business behind it, and we work to that standard from brief to breakdown.

Away-days in the Cotswolds, product launches in Shoreditch, investor dinners in Mayfair. Whatever the format, we handle the logistics so your team can focus on the room.

Read more about corporate events

03

Parties

Milestone birthdays, landmark anniversaries, engagement celebrations, and private dinners. Parties that are designed around the person at the centre of them — their taste, their guest list, their idea of a good evening. We handle the planning so the host can actually be present on the night.

Intimate gatherings and larger celebrations alike. We work with your venue or help you find one.

Read more about private parties

04

Events & Other

Naming ceremonies. Charity fundraisers. Pop-up experiences. If it matters to you and it needs to be right, we're interested in talking about it. We take on a small number of bespoke projects each year that don't fit a standard category but deserve the same level of attention.

Tell us what you're planning — we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
